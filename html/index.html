Welcome to the Heart of Downtown

Lincoln Square Apartments has been in business for other 30 years and is located in the heart of the business district in downtown Springfield, IL. Our family and locally owned apartment community has a number of residents who have made Lincoln Square their home since the very beginning. The community has been voted "Best Apartments" in 2014 by the Illinois Times, as well as the winner of the "Reader's Choice" award from the State Journal Register in 2012, 2013, 2014, 2019, and 2021.
